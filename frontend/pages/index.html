[ FAQ ]

Frequently asked questions.

What services does BhAAi Fans Digital AA offer? +
We offer Web Development, Social Media Marketing, Ads Management (Google & Meta), Video Editing, Graphic Design, and full Marketing Strategy. Each service can be taken individually or bundled.
How long does a typical project take? +
It depends on scope — a website typically takes 3–6 weeks, while ongoing marketing retainers are month-to-month. We'll give you a clear timeline in our first call.
Do you work with startups or only established brands? +
Both! We love working with startups that need to build from scratch just as much as established brands looking to scale or rebrand.
What is your pricing structure? +
We have project-based and monthly retainer packages. See our Pricing page for details, or contact us for a custom quote.
How do I get started? +
Simply fill out the form on our Contact page or send us an email. We'll schedule a free discovery call and take it from there.
